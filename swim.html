❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Is there a lifeguard on duty? +
No — a parent or guardian must be present at all times during all sessions. This is a coaching program, not a supervised swim, and safety is a shared responsibility.
What if my child misses a session? +
Sessions are weather-dependent and full scheduling details will be shared after registration. We'll do our best to accommodate — reach out directly and we'll work something out.
Are private lessons available? +
Yes — for advanced or competitive swimmers, private hourly sessions are available. Reach out separately at info@caryparksolutions.com to discuss availability and rate.
What should my child bring? +
Swimsuit, towel, goggles, and sunscreen. A water bottle is strongly recommended. Please arrive 5 minutes early for the first session.
How is payment handled? +
Payment is accepted by cash or check. Details will be provided after registration confirmation. No payment is required to register.
