The Difference

Why Patients Trust Us

An independent, physician-owned practice built around one purpose: getting you back to what you love.

🏥

Physician-Owned Practice

We answer to our patients — not a hospital system. Independent, focused, and community-rooted since 1991. Every decision is guided by what's best for you.

🎓

Fellowship-Trained Surgeons

Every doctor holds dual board certification in orthopedic surgery AND hand surgery. The highest level of specialized training available in upper extremity care.

📅

Same-Week Appointments

We know pain doesn't wait. Same-day and next-day appointments are frequently available. On-call 24/7 for urgent needs — because injuries don't keep business hours.

💊

Conservative First Approach

Surgery when necessary — never when it isn't. We explore every non-surgical option first and involve you in every decision. Your goals drive the plan.

Integrated Therapy

Integrated Hand-to-Shoulder Therapy

Our therapy department works directly alongside our surgeons — sharing the same space, the same records, and the same commitment to your recovery. From post-operative rehab to non-surgical treatment, our therapists are hand and upper extremity specialists.

  • Certified hand therapists on staff
  • Seamless communication between surgery and therapy teams
  • Custom splinting and orthotic fabrication
  • Post-operative and non-surgical rehabilitation programs
  • Conveniently co-located with our surgical team
