FAQ

Common Questions

Do you offer payment plans?+
Yes — 50% upfront, 50% on launch. For Growth packages we can discuss a 3-payment structure.
What do I need to provide?+
Your logo (if you have one), any brand colors or preferences, the content you want on each page, and a list of what the site needs to accomplish. We'll guide you through the rest.
How fast can you launch?+
Starter sites typically launch in 5–7 business days from content approval. Growth sites take 2–3 weeks. Rush delivery is available — ask us.
Do you offer bilingual (EN/ES) sites?+
Yes — bilingual support is available on all packages. We're natively bilingual and can write, translate, or adapt your content in both languages.
What about ongoing support after launch?+
We offer SLA-backed website support plans. Visit the Support page to open a ticket or inquire about a support retainer.
